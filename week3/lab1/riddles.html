Welcome to the HTML JavaScript Class Riddles Page

  1. What do the letter T and an island have in common?


    You'll find them both in the middle of water.

  2. I'm the rare case when today comes before yesterday. What am I?


    A dictionary.

  3. What goes all the way around the world but stays in a corner?


    A stamp.

  4. You cannot keep me until you have given me. What am I?


    Your word.

  5. What gets bigger the more you take away?


    A hole.

  6. What has four fingers and a thumb, but isn't alive?


    A glove.

  7. If you have me, you will want to share me. If you share me, you will no longer have me. What am I?


    A secret.

  8. What gets wet when drying?


    A towel.

  9. What two words, added together, contain the most letters?


    Post office.

  10. What has a head and a tail, but no body?


    A coin.
